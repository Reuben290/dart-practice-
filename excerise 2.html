chapter 1

The rain had just begun to fall when Kofi noticed the old wooden box sitting beneath the mango tree, its surface worn smooth as if countless hands had touched it before his. He was certain it hadn’t been there that morning. Curious, he knelt down, brushing away damp leaves, and found a small brass latch shaped like a keyhole—but he had no key. As thunder rolled softly in the distance, he hesitated, then pressed his thumb against the latch. To his surprise, it clicked open, and inside, instead of treasure, lay a single folded note with his name written on it—*before he had even been born.*
